About Us
Home to the nerds since 1969

Cepheid Variable stands as a Texas A&M Student Organization devoted to the support and promotion of all things science fiction, fantasy, horror, science, and technology, and the support of the community that grows around them through an atmosphere of acceptance and kinship.
. Cepheid Variable seeks to promote creativity, enrich imagination, and build active learning opportunities through a variety of educational programs, demonstrations, and presentations.

And More!

Check out our small programs!

Roll For Initiative

Roll for initiative is our table top RPG group. Lean to play games like DND and pathfinder, get matched to a group, or join in on our DM workshops.

Card Shop

Card shop is a place to play card games of all types. They hold weekly events for MTG and host competitions and discussions for all sorts of card games

Pencils of Doom

Pencils of Doom is our local art program which, despite the name, focuses on all forms of art media. Wither you do digial or traditional art, jump on in! Take part in our fun monthly and weekly prompts to keep things lively or just ask for art advice. We are welcoming of artist of all levels!

Pokehomies

Pokehomies is where all pokemon fans gather together in different aspects! We have everything from Pokemon Go Days and themed showdown tournaments to TCG and casual play!
